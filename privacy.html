Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PáircPro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our GAA video analysis platform.

Quick Summary: We only collect data necessary to provide our service, never sell your information, and give you control over your data at all times.

By using PáircPro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (required for registration)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Billing information (processed securely by Stripe)
  • Communication preferences

Usage Data

  • Video files you upload for analysis
  • Match analysis data and statistics
  • Event tags and annotations you create
  • App usage patterns and performance metrics

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and screen resolution
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our service:

  • Service Delivery: To provide video analysis features and store your match data
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process payments, and provide support
  • Communication: To send important updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform (anonymized data only)
  • Security: To detect fraud, abuse, and ensure platform security
We never: Sell your data to third parties, use your video content for training AI models, or share your analysis data with other users without permission.

4. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Video files: Secure cloud storage with Cloudflare R2 (EU servers)
  • Analysis data: Supabase database (EU region)
  • All data stored within the European Union for GDPR compliance

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage for video files and analysis data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication available for accounts
  • SOC 2 compliant infrastructure partners

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Video files: Automatically deleted 90 days after account deletion
  • Backup data: Securely deleted within 6 months

5. Your Rights and Choices

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@paircpro.ie or use the data export/deletion tools in your account settings.

6. Information Sharing

We only share your information in limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Cloudflare: Video storage and content delivery
  • Stripe: Payment processing (they never see your video data)
  • Vercel: Web application hosting

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect rights, property, or safety. We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred. You will be notified with choices about your data.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and basic functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Anonymous analytics to improve our service
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may limit functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored within the European Union. When data is transferred outside the EU, we ensure:

  • Adequate protection through adequacy decisions or appropriate safeguards
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with service providers
  • Regular reviews of data protection measures

9. Children's Privacy

PáircPro is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately for deletion.

For GAA youth teams, we recommend adult coaches or managers create and manage accounts with appropriate consent from parents/guardians.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We'll notify you via email for significant changes
  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Give you 30 days notice for material changes affecting your rights

Continued use of PáircPro after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@paircpro.ie
Address: PáircPro, Ireland
Data Protection Officer: dpo@paircpro.ie

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission if you believe we have not handled your data properly.
